What began as a simple photo taken by Patryk Kornatowski (lead vocalist of the album) was turned into an album cover, fueling the creative dumpster fire that is this album.

All songs on this album are produced via the means of either FL Studio (trial version -- we don't have enough money to afford the full release) or Garageband.
